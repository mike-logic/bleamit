BLEAMIT ESP32 Web Flasher

BLEAMIT is a synchronized lighting system built around ESP32 boards using ESP-NOW and BLE. It's designed for theaters, events, art installations, or mobile interaction zones where multiple devices must reflect real-time color states ‚Äî even without traditional Wi-Fi.

You can flash your ESP32 device by choosing the intended role below and clicking "Install."
